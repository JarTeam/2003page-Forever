How did the project begun

It begun as a idea, after i noticed the "Free frontpage extensions" link XPuser (who left the project almost a year later) sent to me, I asked to him if we can do a frontpage website. A reset was made due to the "Open Site" thing, which added useless metadata that no browsers could read.

This wasn't the first time i made one of those "nostalgia" type of sites, I remember making something simillar with Frontpage XP (i think?) in 2018, and i made a project called "Bonzi Revived", which tried to replicate the original Bonzi Buddy website, These two have been lost due to deletion. Bonzi Revived wasn't the first site i made, Back in late-2016, Gamerappa.ga was created, It was a somewhat-poorly managed website that went down in late-2017 due to a arguement with me and the server hoster/provider/administrator.

Why is 2003page not "2003"-y?

This is because the era of late-90s/early-00s has been often revived with sites (Some sites on Neocities for example, and Retrosite as well), i wanted to visit a later era, the mid-to-late-00s (in my words, late-2004 to mid-2009)
